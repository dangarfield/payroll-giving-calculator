Giving Matters

We dont see church as a spectator sport. Were a community, people working together for God and his world. This extends to everything we do and that includes the day-to-day financial realities of our work together, resourcing projects both inside and outside of the church.

If youre a visitor, youll never be pressured to give money to the church. You are welcome to donate if you are moved to do so, but you wont ever leave a service feeling like youve been given the hard-sell.

Anyone committed to the HCC community is encouraged to participate in regular financial giving. Its not just about making sure our bills are paid and Gods work is financed; its about acting out what our church is all about joining together in a shared vision. Giving to the work of the local church is one of the ways we worship God and an exciting act of holy rebellion in a world that prioritises personal wealth above all other pursuits.

HCC manages its finances with integrity and accountability those who give can be confident that their money is being put to good work. Occasionally, we present opportunities to invest in one-off needs and new projects. Watch our website and stay tuned to HCC news for when these special projects arise.

How to give

Give Regularly

If you would like to give to the church on a regular basis, you can easily set up a standing order by filling out our form below. This is our preferred method of giving for regular contributors it helps us to plan and budget realistically.

Tax Efficient Giving

Gift Aid – give more for free!

If you pay tax in the UK, we can claim tax back on anything you give to the church. This means the government tops up any amount you give, at no cost to yourself. Fill out the Gift Aid Declaration on the form below. It makes a massive difference!

Other Ways to Give

On a Sunday

Offerings are taken as part of our worship at each of our Sunday services. Please use the giving envelopes available at the Info Point when giving by cash or cheque; this enables us to make your gift go further through Gift Aid.

Online

You can give now online. We process online giving through give.net head to https://my.give.net/hcc.
